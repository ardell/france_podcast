# Episode ideas — not yet written

Backlog of episodes we've discussed but haven't produced. Each lists the
physical doorway, the through-line, and the recurring theme(s) it carries.
Format, tone, and workflow are defined in `CLAUDE.md`. To produce one, just
ask; it gets written to `episodes/text/NNNN-slug.txt`, built, and published.

Numbering uses four-digit prefixes spaced by ~100 (see `CLAUDE.md`), so a new
idea can slot anywhere in trip order. Everything through the Lyon stop and the
thematic food/wine episodes has now been produced; what remains below is
optional future material.
